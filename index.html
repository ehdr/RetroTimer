Retro Timer

A wonderfully simple, no-nonsense countdown timer for Android

Retro Timer is a wonderfully simple, no-nonsense countdown timer. Inspired by an old-fashioned analog egg timer, you just turn the dial and the alarm is set! To cancel the alarm, just turn it back down to zero.

The feature set has intentionally been kept very small, so it won't distract you from what you are trying to get done. Your only options are to toggle the ringer and vibrator, so you can use it e.g. in an open office environment.

The inspiration for the app came from the Lifehacker article Use a Timer as a Productivity Booster and Sanity Minder.
